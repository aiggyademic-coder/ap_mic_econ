# AP Microeconomics Master Mindmap & Practice Tool üåç

This is a comprehensive, offline-ready study tool for AP Microeconomics, covering all 6 units and integrating the 2025 Free Response Questions (FRQs).

## üöÄ How to Use

1.  **Start Here**: Double-click `index.html` to open the Master Overview in your web browser.
2.  **Explore Units**: Click on any Unit link (e.g., "Go to Unit 1 Mindmap") to see a detailed, interactive map of that unit.
    *   **Zoom/Pan**: Use your mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom in and out. Click and drag to move around.
    *   **Expand/Collapse**: Click on the circles at the end of branches to open or close topics.
3.  **Practice FRQs**: Click "Go to FRQ Practice" or follow links from specific topics to see the 2025 Free Response Questions.
    *   The **Practice Arena** (`practice.html`) organizes questions by unit.
    *   It includes links back to the relevant mindmap sections for review.

## üìö What's Included?

*   **Master Dashboard** (`index.html`): High-level view of exam weights and unit importance.
*   **6 Unit Mindmaps**:
    *   Unit 1: Basic Economic Concepts
    *   Unit 2: Supply & Demand
    *   Unit 3: Production, Cost & Perfect Competition
    *   Unit 4: Imperfect Competition (Monopoly, Oligopoly, etc.)
    *   Unit 5: Factor Markets
    *   Unit 6: Market Failure & Government
*   **Practice Arena**: All 6 FRQs from the 2025 exam sets, tagged and linked to content.

## üéí Teacher/Student Notes

*   **Offline Ready**: This entire folder works without an internet connection. You can put it on a USB drive or save it to your laptop.
*   **Exam Focused**: Content is weighted based on AP Exam percentages. High-yield topics like "Monopoly" and "Supply & Demand" have more detail.
*   **Visual Learning**: Designed for students who learn better with spatial organization than linear text.
